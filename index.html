About Me

I am an assistant professor of Psychology at St. Lawrence University.

I study culture and language.

Research

  • I am drawn to elegant, principled approaches that are both practical and scalable, with broad relevance in real-world settings.
  • Currently, I am actively thinking about:
  • What aspects of our culture facilitate or hinder connections to oneself, others, and nature?
  • How do these connections, or lack thereof, impact our psychology?
  • What interventions can effectively foster meaningful connection?
